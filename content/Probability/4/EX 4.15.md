${z}_{.05}$ is the ${100}\left( {1 - {.05}}\right)$ th $= {95}$ th percentile of the standard normal distribution, so ${z}_{.05} = {1.645}$. 
The area under the standard normal curve to the left of $- {z}_{.05}$ is also .05.
